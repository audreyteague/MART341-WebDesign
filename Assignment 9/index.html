Important Ideas When Plannig Your Wedding

I am going through the wedding planning process right now and the idea of having a webite with the information easily accessible made me create this website for other people.


Biggest Parts Of Your Wedding

  1. Photographer
  2. Catering
  3. Guest List
  4. Budget
  5. Location
  6. Time of Year
  7. Style

Budget

The budget is THE MOST important step. Go to toolkit tab for a better in depth walkthrough of budgeting for your big day. This can impact your size guest list and the size of your wedding party. This can also impact your catering options like meal size and bar size.

Location and Time Of Year

Once your budget has been established. Talk with your partner about where you may want to have your wedding. This can help guide you towards finding venues that are within your price range. Another factor you have to consider is time of year which may impact cost and overall budget.

Photographer

When your location is set and time of year, you can contact and look into photographers that you may want to work with and check availability.
